Site Plan for Discover Japan

Site Name

Discover Japan

This name encapsulates the essence of the website, which is to explore and learn about the various aspects of Japan, from its rich culture and history to its modern attractions and culinary delights. The domain is discover-japan.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of "Discover Japan" is to provide a comprehensive guide to Japan, offering valuable information for tourists, cultural enthusiasts, and anyone interested in learning more about the country. The site will feature detailed articles on Japanese culture, travel guides to must-visit places, an exploration of Japanese cuisine, and practical tips for travelers.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A traveler planning a trip to Japan wants to know the best places to visit and the must-see attractions in Tokyo.

    Content: A detailed travel guide to Tokyo, highlighting popular tourist spots, hidden gems, and travel tips.

  • Scenario 2: A cultural enthusiast is interested in learning about traditional Japanese festivals and customs.

    Content: Articles and insights on various Japanese festivals, their significance, and how they are celebrated.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #B22222 (Japanese Red) - Headers, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (White) - Backgrounds and text.
  • Accent Color: #000000 (Black) - Text and other accents.
  • Header Background: #808080 (Gray) - Background for header.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Noto Serif JP', serif - All headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.)
  • Body Font: 'Noto Sans JP', sans-serif - Paragraph text and general content
  • Special Sections: 'Pacifico', cursive - Special highlights or quotes
